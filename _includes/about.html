About

Schoolhouse Labs is part of Founder Landon's journey into education and web development.

  • 2011-2013

    Middle School Teacher

    Landon taught 6th through 8th grade math with Teach for America in Colorado Springs. As a teacher, he saw the power of tech to engage students and make himself more efficient as a teacher.

  • June 2013

    Web Developer

    Knowing that the best way to become a part of the edtech movement, Landon started learning Ruby on Rails and became a web developer to be able to build web apps.

  • August 2014

    Creation of Schoolhouse Labs

    Schoolhouse Labs is helping teacher entrepreneurs and schools get their ideas into the classroom.
